Visit Khartoum!

A hidden gem ready to be discovered

Attractions for visitors in Khartoum:

Tuti Island

Explore the island, and see where the Blue Nile and White Nile merge to form the main Nile river!

The Nubian Pyramids

Learn about the ancient Kushite kingdoms, and enjoy the view of the pyramids!

The Grand Mosque

See the largest, and one of the oldest mosques in Sudan!
